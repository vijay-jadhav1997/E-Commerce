About

Know More About Us

Welcome to Indic Products App, your one-stop platform for buying and selling authentic and quality products!

Our mission is to empower individuals, artisans, and small businesses across India to showcase their unique products to a wider audience. Whether you're a seller with handcrafted goods, traditional attire, or modern gadgets, or a buyer looking for quality products with an Indian essence, Indic Products App bridges the gap between buyers and sellers effortlessly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions About Indic Products

What is Indic Products App?

pIndic Products App is a platform where users can buy and sell various products, ranging from handmade crafts to everyday essentials, by creating their own accounts.

How do I create an account?

Click on the Sign Up button, fill in your details, and verify your email to start using the platform.

Is Indic Products App free to use?

Yes, creating an account and browsing products is completely free. However, a small commission may apply to sellers for successful transactions.

How do I place an order?

Browse the product categories, select the product you want, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

What payment methods are available?

We support various payment options, including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets.

Is shopping on Indic Products App secure?

Absolutely! We use secure payment gateways and ensure data privacy for all users.
